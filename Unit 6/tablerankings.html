The following is a table recognizing my best placements in the world on 'Track of the Day's. The Track of the Day is a daily time placement tournament where the community races to set the best time on a selected map made by the community within 24 hours. After 24 hours, the official leaderboards are locked and in-game trophies are distributed. Hence, why I call this list my "best times," since my best times resulted in my best placements. On average, 8,000 to 9,000 people will play a Track of the Day before leaderboards close, and since starting in April 2022, I have played just under 400 Track of the Days (I try to do it daily.)
